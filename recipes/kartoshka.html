“Kartoshka” Cake (No-Bake Chocolate Dessert)

A rich, naturally sweet treat made from dates, cocoa, and coconut. Soft, chocolatey, and shaped like a mini cake or truffle — perfect with tea or coffee!

Ingredients

  • 60g dates
  • 100ml coconut milk
  • 60-70g flaxseed porridge
  • 30g rice flour
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • A pinch of salt
  • A few drops of vanilla or rum extract

Preparation

  1. Soak the dates in warm water for about 10 minutes if they are dry, then drain and blend them into a paste.
  2. In a bowl, combine the date paste with coconut milk and mix well.
  3. Add flaxseed porridge, rice flour, cocoa powder, and a pinch of salt. Mix until a thick, moldable dough forms.
  4. Add coconut oil and a few drops of vanilla or rum extract for extra flavor. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Shape the mixture into small oval or round pieces resembling mini cakes or truffles.
  6. Optional: roll the cakes in more cocoa powder or shredded coconut for coating.
  7. Chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour to firm up before serving.
