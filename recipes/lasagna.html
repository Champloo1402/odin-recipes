Lasagna

Hearty, cheesy, and layered with flavor, this classic lasagna recipe combines rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients

For meat sauce

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic gloves
  • 500g ground beef
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the béchamel sauce

  • 50g butter
  • 50g all purpose flour
  • 600ml milk
  • Pinch of salt

Layers

  • 9-12 Lasagna sheets
  • 200g shredded mozzarella
  • 100g grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan. Add chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and sugar. Simmer for 20–30 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, make the béchamel sauce: Melt butter in a saucepan, stir in flour, and cook for 1 minute. Gradually whisk in milk and cook until thickened. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  6. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions if not using oven-ready noodles. Drain and set aside.
  7. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish, add a layer of noodles, spread béchamel sauce, and sprinkle mozzarella. Repeat layers, finishing with béchamel and a generous sprinkle of Parmesan on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake uncovered for another 15–20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
